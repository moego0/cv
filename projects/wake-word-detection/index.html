An end-to-end TinyML audio pipeline that trains custom keyword recognition models using heavy audio data augmentation and deploys them on microcontrollers under extreme RAM limitations.

System Architecture

Raw audio input -> Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC) feature extraction -> 2D CNN classification -> Post-training INT8 quantization -> TFLite Micro micro-controller execution.

Technical Challenges

Dealing with noisy environments and limited sample recordings for custom commands. Resolved by writing a custom pipeline to synthesize noise variables and pitch deviations.

Results & Impact

Achieved 98% accuracy on custom voice targets under test conditions. Successfully compressed models to fit under 80KB storage size, ensuring they fit easily in STM32F4/ESP32 internal flash memory.

Lessons Learned

Gained a deep understanding of mathematical signal analysis (Fourier transforms) and fixed-point neural networks.
